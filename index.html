📖 How to Tell a Story — Just Two Steps!

  1. 1Press the big green button and start talking. Take your time — laugh, pause, tell it your way!
  2. 2When you're done, press the red button. That's it! It saves everything automatically.

You don't need to do anything else. The app types out what you said and backs it all up by itself.

☁️ Where Your Stories Go

Every story is saved in two places automatically:

  • Right inside this app — tap "My Stories" to read and listen
  • In a folder called "Grandma's Stories" in your Google Drive, with both the text and an MP3 recording of your voice

Your family can listen and read from anywhere in the world!

🔑 Getting Your OpenAI Key

  • Go to platform.openai.com and create a free account
  • Add a small amount of credit (e.g. $5 — lasts a very long time)
  • Click your name → API Keys → Create new secret key
  • Copy it and paste into Step 1 at the top of this page
  • You only ever need to do this once

🔑 Setting Up Google Drive (one time)

  • Go to console.cloud.google.com, create a free project called "Grandma Stories"
  • Enable the Google Drive API
  • Create an OAuth 2.0 Client ID (Web application type)
  • Add your file's URL to Authorized JavaScript Origins
  • Paste the Client ID into the GOOGLE_CLIENT_ID line in this file
  • Then click "Sign in with Google" — done forever!
